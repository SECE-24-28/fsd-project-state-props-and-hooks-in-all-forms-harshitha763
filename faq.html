Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about FashionCart

Orders & Shopping
How do I place an order?
Browse our collections, add products to your cart, proceed to checkout, fill in your delivery address, choose a payment method and confirm. You'll receive an order confirmation instantly.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
You can cancel your order from the "My Orders" page as long as the status is "Confirmed". Once it's been shipped, cancellation is not possible. For modifications, please contact our support team immediately after placing the order.
How do I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" in your account. You'll see the current status of your order — Confirmed, Processing, Shipped, or Delivered — along with a visual progress tracker.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders up to ₹10,000.
Delivery & Shipping
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Metro cities typically receive orders within 2–3 days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select pin codes at an additional charge.
Is free shipping available?
Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹499. Orders below ₹499 have a flat shipping fee of ₹50.
Do you ship to all pin codes in India?
We deliver to 25,000+ pin codes across India. If your pin code is not serviceable, you'll be notified at checkout.
Returns & Refunds
What is the return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused, unwashed, with original tags and packaging intact. Some categories like lingerie and customized items are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
How do I initiate a return?
Go to "My Orders", select the order, click "Return Item" and follow the instructions. Our team will arrange a pickup within 2 business days. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the item is received.
When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are credited back to your original payment method within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. For COD orders, refunds are issued as store credits or NEFT transfer.
Account & Security
How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" on the homepage, fill in your name, email, phone number and create a password. Your account is ready instantly — no email verification required.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to reset your password.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all transactions. Your passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We never share your personal data with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you.
